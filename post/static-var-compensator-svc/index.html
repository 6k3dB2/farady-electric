Static Var Compensator (SVC)

Email your specs

General

Farady FDWK type line reactive power automatic compensation series device is used for 6kV~33kV lines. It uses microcomputer technology & Vacuum contactor t o automatic control by switching on & off shunt capacitor banks according to the parameters of voltage, time, power factor and reactive power to improve line power factor, reduce line consumption and improve voltage quality.

Service conditions

◆ installation location: outdoor, no violent mechanical vibration, no harmful gas and steam, no conductive or explosive dust;

◆ altitude: ≤ 2000m;

◆ ambient temperature: - 25 ℃ ~ + 45 ℃;

◆ wind speed: no more than 34m / S;

◆ seismic intensity: no more than 8 degrees;

◆ relative humidity: daily average not more than 95%, monthly average not more than 90%;

◆ special usage environment, feel free to contact Farady technical dept
